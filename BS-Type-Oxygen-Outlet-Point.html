Product Description

BS Type Oxygen Outlet Point is a medical gas valve intended to provide oxygen to patients in hospitals. To give patients a safe and dependable source of oxygen, it is often installed in the gas distribution system of a hospital or other medical facility. The British Standard (BS) type oxygen output point is made to work with medical gas systems that adhere to the British Standard (BS). It is often constructed from premium materials that are strong, corrosion-resistant, and able to bear high pressures, like brass or stainless steel.
