ABOUT US

Medplus Hospital Solutions was established in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, in 2021 and since then it has been providing a reliable range of various medical equipment such as Hospital Wall Guard, Cardiac Monitor Stand, Hospital Cubicle Curtain Track, Single Gauge Oxygen Gas Regulator, Amico Type Oxygen Outlet Point, and many others. We operate in this sector of the industry as a competent manufacturer, trader, exporter, service provider and supplier. Customers appreciate our products, which motivates us to do even better with each passing day. We are a promising organization working with dignity and responsibility, fully committed to providing the top quality products to our clients. We have specialized in this domain in a very short period of time and continue to innovate our product range to keep in line with industry trends. We are serving the best interests of each customer which helps us earn the respect in the operating domain. Our team of highly experienced professionals is our backbone, and their innovative and creative ideas do wonders for us. They are experienced and informed about the recent development in the sector. We are continually investigating prospective markets in order to expand our reach throughout the nation.
