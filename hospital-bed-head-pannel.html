Product Description

Hospital Bed Head Panel is a specialized piece of medical equipment that is used in hospital rooms to offer patients and medical staff a variety of features and functionality. These panels serve several purposes, including electrical outlets, nurse call buttons, lighting controls, and medical gas outlets, and are normally situated on the head of the bed. With features like adjustable height and tilt, hospital bed head panels are made to be extremely flexible and configurable to offer optimal placement for the patient and healthcare provider.
